What are affirmations and how to use them

A powerful way to reprogram your mind.

Posted by Noemi & Armando on August 24, 2021

Affirmations are a tool that assists in shifting our perception from negative thoughts/feelings to positive ones. Think of affirmations as weights to tone the muscles in our brains and help maintain the mind fit. The more we use them, the more we can see good results in our lives.

Many times, our thoughts aren't pleasant. We can think the worse about ourselves at work, in a relationship, about our dreams, and even ourselves. By the time we get to adulthood, it can be challenging to change our mindset to experience a balanced life and not affect our mental health. Affirmations can help ease the internal chaos. It doesn't mean that challenges will disappear, but we can develop the inner alignment and courage to face our dark days and grow from them.

It's time to live in the moment, don't think about the past or worry about the future.

Daily affirmations can inspire and motivate us to keep moving forward in our journey. They help us to get mentally fit, to develop muscles in the brain that produce positive energy, thus allowing our hearts to be filled with hopes.
